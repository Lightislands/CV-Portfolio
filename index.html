What I do


UX/UI Design

A great UX design is a key to the successful application. Market competition is high and making customer to stay is extremely important. UX is about taking care of people's experience, that's why gathering an information and talking to people is so important here.
UI design should be clear and intuitive. "Keep it simple" is a principle that works well here.

Interactive prototyping

Everything has to be tested. It's better to touch something, in order to understand it better. That's where interactive prototypes are being used. To test or get feedback I create prototypes using Axure or inVision. If something doesn't meet the expectations - it has to be re-done, until perfect.

Front End development

Responsive web design and Front End development. Focusing on writing clean, elegant and efficient code. Using HTML, CSS, CMS and touch of jQuery.

Competitive analysis


Performing a review of competing websites and applications in order to understand industry standards and find out areas for improvements or innovations in given area, preparing report that summarizes the competitive landscape.

Capturing user stories and expectations. Groupping them logically in pillars. Mapping out product features for each pillar, identifying gaps and opportunities.
